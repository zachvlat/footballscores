Scorito

A modern Android app for tracking live match scores and match results.

<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Real-time match scores and live status updates</li>
<li>Team/Competition search functionality to filter matches by team names</li>
<li>Date navigation (today, yesterday, tomorrow)</li>
<li>Matches organized by competition/league</li>
<li>Match details including team logos, scores, and status</li>
</ul>
